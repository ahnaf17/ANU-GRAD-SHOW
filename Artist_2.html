ANITA GREWAL

PHOTOGRAPHY

BACHELOR OF VISUAL ARTS

Born in London and raised and educated in India from the age of nine, I started my professional life as a school teacher. After spending twelve years teaching and living in Delhi, Hastings, Bogota, New York and Munich I moved permanently to Australia in 2005 determined to try something different. After teaching English in Perth, donning hard hat and steel capped boots in the Pilbara and training forensic scientists in Canberra I found myself back in education but this time as a student not a teacher. I took up visual arts for a number of reasons, but the main reason has been the simple joy of experimenting and creating. I am currently exploring the pleasures of abstract photography and mixed media collage.
