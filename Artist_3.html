HAZEL GROVE

PHOTOGRAPHY

BACHELOR OF VISUAL ARTS

From the moment I picked up my first camera, I fell head over heels for photography. I started pursuing photography in 2010 and it has taken me many places since. I’m particularly interested in photographing people, revealing personalities through portraits and the way we respond to portraits.Growing up in a rural town meant I would have to relocate for further study in photography. In 2014 I moved to Canberra to undertake a Bachelor of Visual Arts majoring in Photomedia at Australian National University’s School of Art. In 2016 I completed my Bachelor Degree and now I am currently studying Honours in Photomedia also at the ANU. My Honours project specialises in wet-plate Collodion photography, an old photographic medium which predates digital and film. With this medium I will photograph Cosplayers and observe the interaction of a very old process and a relatively very new culture. I have both commercial and artistic practices. In my spare time currently I work as a freelance photographer, which sponsors my artistic pursuit.
